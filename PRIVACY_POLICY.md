# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

At **OPM (Orpheus Music)**, your privacy is our highest priority. We built OPM to give you absolute control over your music experience without compromising your personal data. 

This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage. By using OPM, you agree to the practices described below.

---

## Our Core Privacy Principles

1. **No Tracking:** We do not track your listening habits across other apps or websites.
2. **No Ads:** OPM is a 100% ad-free experience. We will never serve advertisements or share your data with advertisers.
3. **No Personal Data Collection:** We do not require you to create an account, nor do we collect identifiable personal information (like your name, email, or phone number).
4. **Privacy First:** All listening history, playlists, and cached tracks are stored locally on your device using an encrypted local database.
5. **Open Source Transparency:** Every single line of code powering OPM is public and available for anyone to audit on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/sauravthakurq/opm).

---

## Information Collection & Usage

### 1. Minimal Diagnostics (Optional)
To help us improve app stability, we optionally collect minimal, anonymized crash reports and diagnostic data (via Firebase Analytics). This data is strictly used to fix bugs and improve performance. It is never sold or used for marketing.

### 2. Third-Party Music APIs
OPM connects directly from your device to third-party streaming APIs (such as YouTube Music or Spotify) to stream audio and fetch metadata. We act only as a client interface. When you stream music, your IP address is exposed to these third-party providers just as it would be if you visited their websites directly in your browser. 

We do not route your traffic through any middleman servers.

---

## Data Security

Your data stays entirely on your device. We do not maintain central servers containing user data, which means there is no central database for malicious actors to breach. 

## International Users

If you are using OPM outside of your home country, please note that third-party streaming services you connect to may process metadata in regions where their servers are located.
